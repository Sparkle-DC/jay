Jayathmi D. Gamachchige serves as Assistant Manager — Commercial & Marketing at Hambantota International Port Group (HIPG), a strategic public-private partnership between the Government of Sri Lanka and China Merchants Port Holdings (CMPort).

Based at the port's commercial and marketing division, Jayathmi is responsible for coordinating foreign delegations and embassy visits, managing port visit coordination, and supporting the broader commercial marketing function. She works alongside the DGM-Commercial & Marketing and a team of commercial executives to promote HIPG's diverse service portfolio — including RO-RO, container cargo, breakbulk, energy services, and marine services.

Her international education in transportation management and logistics gives her a unique vantage point on global trade corridors, making her an effective bridge between HIPG and its international stakeholders.
