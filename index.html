Jayathmi Gamachchige is a Commercial Marketing Manager at Hambantota International Port (HIP) — a deep-water, multi-purpose port strategically located 10 nautical miles from the world's busiest east-west shipping route.

She brings a results-driven approach to marketing strategy, client relationship management, and business development in the port and maritime sector. Her work spans RO-RO, container cargo, energy services, and marine services, connecting global partners to Sri Lanka's most diversified port.

With a deep understanding of the Indian Ocean trade corridor and the port's role as a transshipment hub for over 700,000 vehicles per month, Jayathmi helps drive HIP's commercial vision and market positioning.
